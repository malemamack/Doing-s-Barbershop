Services Provided

Complete Grooming Package

Cut or Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and timeless hairstyle that exudes confidence and individuality.

Cut + Dye

The Cut and Dye combination is a powerful way to transform your look, blending a fresh haircut with vibrant or subtle hair coloring.

Cut + Bleach

Bleaching is a chemical process that removes the natural pigment from your hair, resulting in a lighter base color. It’s often used to achieve a platinum blonde or to prepare the hair for vibrant dyeing.

Cut + Bleach + Coloure

The Cut + Bleach + Color service is the ultimate transformation for your hair, combining a sharp haircut, lightening through bleaching, and vibrant or subtle coloring.

Brush

Hair brushing is a simple yet essential grooming routine that plays a key role in maintaining healthy and stylish hair

Cheskop

The Chiskop (or "Cheskop") is a classic, low-maintenance haircut that involves shaving the hair very close to the scalp, leaving a clean and polished look. Popular in African cultures, this style is timeless, practical, and versatile, often chosen for its neat appearance and ease of upkeep.

Bleach only

Hair bleaching uses a chemical formula (typically a combination of bleach powder and developer) to lift the natural pigments in your hair, resulting in a lighter shade.
